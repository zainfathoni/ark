Visual brief for Wheeljack

The Dockyard Icon

A Telegram group avatar that feels like a sibling to The Ark: dark, cinematic, amber-lit, and instantly readable at tiny chat-icon size.

512×512 SVG source Telegram circular crop safe Ark-style amber glow Harbor / shipyard metaphor

Concept: anchor = stable ACP port, crane = active workshop, waves = Dockyard/Ark family.

Design direction

  • 1Sibling, not clone. Keep The Ark’s dark sci-fi + amber language, but switch the motif from spaceship/orbit to harbor/shipyard.
  • 2Readable in Telegram. Use one dominant symbol: anchor, crane, or combined anchor-crane. Avoid small text inside the avatar.
  • 3ACP meaning. The Dockyard is where work gets routed, repaired, and launched — stable port, active cranes, clean channels.

SVG anatomy

01 / Background

Rounded square, nearly black navy, subtle radial amber glow from center.

02 / Ring

Thin orbital/dock perimeter ring to visually link it to The Ark icon.

03 / Main mark

Large amber anchor, or anchor fused with a simple crane silhouette.

04 / Waterline

Low, soft cyan wave band. Keep it secondary so the anchor stays dominant.
